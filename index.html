Our Overview

Rafiki Mental Health Awareness Initiative provides a range of services designed to support individuals facing mental health challenges. Our trained professionals work collaboratively with clients to foster resilience, encourage self-discovery, and promote emotional well-being.

We believe that mental health is as important as physical health, and we strive to create a safe space where everyone can feel heard, understood, and valued.
